3D Art

In four years of live-action film and animated TV, I’ve worked at two different studios, with clients like Netflix, Apple, Peacock, and PBS. Specializing in lighting, texturing, and compositing, I’ve contributed to projects featuring high-profile individuals, such as Adam Sandler, Dua Lipa, and Al Roker. I’m proficient in Maya, Houdini, Katana, Nuke, and Unreal Engine, and experienced with Renderman, Redshift, and Arnold. I take initiative to complete, optimize, and troubleshoot shots using strong artistic, organizational and coding skills. My training includes Graduate Certificates in Computer Animation and in Visual Effects from Sheridan College. See the projects I've been a part of below!
