Project Details


Argylle is an action-comedy film with an ensemble cast that includes Henry Cavill, Dua Lipa, Bryce Dallas Howard, and Sam Rockwell. In this project I was primarily tasked with setting up and managing the lighting and rendering for a chase sequence that takes place in the Greek town of Ios. The sequence involved a large CG environment, vehicles, and digital doubles, as well as various dust and destruction FX. I worked closely with my team lead, our CG Supervisor, and VFX Supervisor, to achieve director Matthew Vaughn’s vision for the sequence, while ensuring continuity across this complex series of shots. I also had some additional work lighting face replacements over stunt doubles for dance and fight sequences featuring Bryce Dallas Howard and Sam Rockwell’s characters.

During my time on this project, I served as Sequence Lead, a role in which I supported junior artists, helped prioritize tasks, and contributed to workflow improvements, gaining valuable experience balancing multiple responsibilities under tight deadlines.
Lighting for Argylle was carried out in Katana, rendered with Renderman, and composited in Nuke.
