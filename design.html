Design

I specialize in brand, print, and motion design, with over 10 years of experience across creative studios and educational institutions. I hold an Honours Bachelor’s Degree in Design, and my solid artistic principles that I've developed over time are what formed the foundation for my later work in 3D, film, and television.

My design experience includes studio roles at Underline and Underdog Studio/Wasabi Media in Toronto, contributing to branding, print, UI/UX, and motion projects for clients such as DraftKings, Molson Coors, and Kubota. I’ve also worked with York University and Sheridan College, and served as Director of Design for a TEDx conference. Samples of my work with explanations can be viewed below.
