Social & Web

In addition to my chosen fields of focus in design (brand, print, and motion), I also have a experience working on web and social media projects. This spans from designing, developing, and maintain websites, to conceptualizing and managing feeds for various organizations and projects.

Such experience includes being a Digital Content Specialist for Sheridan College, pitching site ideas for both Sheridan and Underdog Studio, and developing entire web pages for small businesses and blogs. I have worked with Content Management Systems (Sitecore, WordPress), Web Builder and Hosting Services (Squarespace, Wix), and have also created websites from the ground up, in HTML, CSS and JS.
